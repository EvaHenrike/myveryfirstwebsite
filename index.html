Here are some topics that move me and that deserve more attention than my person. Therefore I use this important place on my very first front page to focus on really important topics that often receive too little attention elsewhere. Enjoy and stay curious.

Clicks with consequences.

Sustainability has many faces. The efficient use of digital gadgets is one of them. One hour of video streaming produces about as much CO2 as one kilometer of driving a car. The climate gas is also produced by search queries: According to Google, one query produces about 0.2 grams of CO2.

Eat your veggies.

Every German consumed just under 60 kilos of meat on average last year. According to a study, meat consumption in Germany results in annual costs of just under six billion euros that do not appear on the price tag. Go Vegan.

Biodiversity is everything.

Sustainable development involves more than just climate protection. According to the United Nations report on biodiversity, up to 130 animal and plant species now die every day. Protect our ecosystems!

Support diversity.

Sustainability and diversity belong together. Diversity is a concept used in sociology and social psychology for the distinction and recognition of group and individual characteristics. Take a step back and make space for more diversity.
